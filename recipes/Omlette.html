Omlette

Ingridients:

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1-2 tablespoons milk or cream (optional, for fluffiness)
  • 1 tablespoon butter or oil
  • 1/4 cup diced vegetables (onions, bell peppers, tomatoes, mushrooms, etc.)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Ingredients:

    Crack the eggs into a bowl. Add a pinch of salt, pepper, and the milk or cream if using. Whisk until the mixture is smooth and slightly frothy. Prepare your fillings: grate the cheese, dice the vegetables, and chop the cooked meat if using.

  2. Heat the Pan:

    Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Add the butter or oil to the pan, letting it melt and coat the surface evenly.

  3. Cook the Eggs:

    Pour the beaten eggs into the pan, tilting the pan to spread the eggs evenly. Let the eggs cook undisturbed for about 1-2 minutes until the edges start to set but the center is still slightly runny.

  4. Add Fillings and Fold:

    Gently fold the other half of the omelette over the fillings using a spatula. Cook for another 1-2 minutes, until the cheese is melted and the eggs are fully cooked. Slide the omelette onto a plate and serve hot.
