Butter Naan

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/4 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter (for the dough)
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter (for brushing)
  • Warm water (as needed)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Dough:

    In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and sugar. Add the yogurt, milk, and 2 tablespoons of melted butter. Mix until the dough starts to come together. Gradually add warm water, a little at a time, kneading the dough until it becomes soft and smooth. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for about 30 minutes.

  2. Divide and Shape the Dough:

    After resting, divide the dough into equal-sized balls (about 6-8 balls). Roll each ball out into an oval or round shape, about 1/4-inch thick.

  3. Cook the Naan:

    Heat a skillet or tawa over medium-high heat. Place a rolled-out dough on the hot skillet. Cook for about 1-2 minutes until bubbles start to form on the surface. Flip the naan and cook the other side for another 1-2 minutes until golden brown spots appear.

  4. Brush with Butter:

    Once cooked, remove the naan from the skillet and brush it with melted butter. Serve hot with your favorite curry or dish.
